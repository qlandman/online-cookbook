## Ingredienten
---

* `500 g` 00 bloem
* `2 g` instant gist
* `10 g` zout
* `300 ml` water


## Bereiding
---

* sunflower oil

> Grease up a medium-size springform and fill in the batter.

---

> Preheat your oven to `180 °C` and bake for `35-40 minutes`.
