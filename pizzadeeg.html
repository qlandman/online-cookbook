Pizzadeeg

  • 4 pizza’s
  • 50 minutes
  • Source
  • Vegan

Basisrecept pizzadeeg met gist

  • 500 g 00 bloem
  • 2 g instant gist
  • 10 g zout
  • 300 ml water

Bereiding

  • sunflower oil

Grease up a medium-size springform and fill in the batter.

Preheat your oven to 180 °C and bake for 35-40 minutes.

Enjoy!
